Rule 2720/2 (Power=2.0)
The Grand Duumvirate

      A person is a Dictator if and only if, on 20 April 2026, that
      person was either the Referee or the Assessor.
      
      A Dictator CAN by announcement grant any asset to any person
      (including emself), or revoke any asset from any person (including
      emself).
      
      A Dictator CAN, with the consent of a specified person, grant a
      specified patent title to that person.
      
      If no Dictator has yet done so, any Dictator can by announcement
      Declare a Junta. Upon doing so, all Dictators win the game (the
      victory method should be recorded as being via Junta, as is
      typical of victories obtained by dictatorships).
      
      A Dictator CAN with notice and with the consent of all other
      Dictators cause this rule to perform a specified Rule Change.
