Rule 2659/14 (Power=1)
Stamps

      Stamps are a category of asset ownable by players and contracts.
      The Collector is a sortitioned office. The Collector tracks Stamps
      in eir weekly report.
      
      For each person there is a corresponding type of stamp. Stamps of
      any given type are fungible.
      
      Any player CAN, once per week, pay X stamps, where each specified
      stamp is a different type, to gain (X-1)*3 radiance.
      
      Any player CAN, once per week, pay X Stamps, where each Stamp is
      the same type, to gain (X-1)*2 radiance.
      
      Any active player CAN Profligately Pursue Philately by paying N
      Stamps, where N is the current number of active players and each
      specified Stamp is of a different type. When e does so, e wins the
      game.
      
      Any player CAN pay a fee of 5 Spendies to grant emself X stamps of
      eir own type. When less than 8 Stamps of eir type exist, X is 2.
      When 8 to 15 Stamps of eir type exist, X is 1. When 16 or more
      stamps of eir type exist, X is 0.
      
      Any player CAN pay a fee of 6 Spendies to transfer a specified
      stamp from the L&FD to emself.
