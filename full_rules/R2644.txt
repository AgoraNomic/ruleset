Rule 2644/0 (Power=2)
The Gauntlet

      When a player makes a correct announcement that a single specified
      player owns 5 or more stones, the specified player Wields the
      Guantlet.
      
      When a player Wields the Gauntlet, e wins the game and all
      existing stones are transferred to Agora.
