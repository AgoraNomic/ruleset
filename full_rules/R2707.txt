Rule 2707/3 (Power=1.0)
Pyrite

      Pyrite is a type of fixed currency, tracked by the Land Managor.
      
      A player CAN pay a fee of 1 Spendie to gain 100 Pyrite.
      
      A player CAN pay a fee of 200 Pyrite to gain 1 Spendie, provided
      that e has gained fewer than 40 Spendies in this way during this
      Agoran month.
      
      A player CAN pay a fee of 1 number card to gain pyrite equal to
      the number of the card's type, or 10 pyrite if it was a 0 card.
