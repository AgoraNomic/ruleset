Rule 2711/1 (Power=1.0)
Light Overexposure

      When a player wins the game as a result of Outshining the Sun, the
      following happen in order:
      
      * All Pyrite is destroyed.
      
      * The Fields Wither.
      
      * All Hectares are destroyed.
      
      * Each player is granted 16 Hectares.
