Rule 2714/0 (Power=1.0)
Spending Number Cards

      A player CAN, once per week, pay ten number cards, where each of
      those number cards is of a different type, to gain 25 radiance.
      
      For any two decimal digits X and Y (which may be the same), a
      player CAN pay 2 spendies, a number card of type X, and a number
      card of type Y to grant emself a number card whose type is the
      last digit of the sum X+Y.

History:

Enacted by P9270 'The Number Cards Game' (ais523), 10 Dec 2025
