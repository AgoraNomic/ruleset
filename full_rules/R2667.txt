Rule 2667/0 (Power=1)
Succumbing to Time

      Once per month, a player CAN Succumb by announcement. When a
      player Succumbs, each of eir deadlines to perform an Officer's
      duty or judge a Call for Judgement is extended by 1 day if it
      would otherwise expire within the next week.
