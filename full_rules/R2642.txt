Rule 2642/12 (Power=2)
Stone Cost

      Stone Cost is a Stone switch with values of non-negative integers
      and a default of 10. Stone Cost is tracked by the Stonemason.
      
      Any player CAN pay a fee of X Spendies to transfer a specified
      non-immune stone to emself, where X is the current Stone Cost of
      the specified stone. When this occurs, the stone cost of that
      stone is set to its default value.
      
      At the beginning of each Agoran week, the Stone Cost of each stone
      is decreased by 1 (to a minimum of 1). When a stone is transferred
      to Agora, its Stone Cost is reset to its default value.
