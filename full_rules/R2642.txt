Rule 2642/0 (Power=2)
Distributing Stones

      The Stonemason CAN initiate an auction for any set of stones
      belonging to Agora for which an auction is not ongoing, with each
      individual stone being an auction lot. The Stonemason is the
      auctioneer, and the currency is coins.
      
      The Stonemason SHALL so initiate an auction for a set of stones
      consisting of at least one third the stones eligible for auction
      in a timely fashion after publishing a collection notice, provided
      there any eligible stones.
