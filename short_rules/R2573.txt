Rule 2573/2 (Power=2)
Impeachment

      Any player CAN, with 2 Agoran consent, expel (impeach) the holder
      of a specified elected office. When a person is impeached from an
      office, an election is immediately opened for that office. Players
      SHOULD NOT use this method of removal unless the officer has
      abused the powers of eir office or otherwise shown emself unworthy
      the trust of Agora.
