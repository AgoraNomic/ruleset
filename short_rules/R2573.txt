Rule 2573/5 (Power=2)
Impeachment

      Any player CAN, with 2 Agoran consent, expel (impeach) the holder
      of a specified voluntary office, causing the office to become
      vacant. When a person is impeached from an office, the selection
      method for that office is immediately initiated (if possible).
      Players SHOULD NOT use this method of removal unless the officer
      has abused the powers of eir office or otherwise shown emself
      unworthy of the trust of Agora.
