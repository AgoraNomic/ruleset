Rule 2492/7 (Power=1)
Recusal

      A judge CAN recuse emself from a CFJ e is assigned to by
      announcement.
      
      The Arbitor CAN recuse a judge from a case by announcement, if
      that judge has violated a time limit for judging the case and has
      not judged it in the mean time; the Arbitor SHALL do so in a
      timely fashion after the time limit expires, if able. The Arbitor
      CAN recuse an inactive or unregistered judge by announcement.
      
      If a judge is recused from a case that was continuously open and
      assigned to em for at least the past 4 days, e SHOULD NOT be
      assigned as a judge until e has apologised and/or reasonably
      explained eir actions.
