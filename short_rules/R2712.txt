Rule 2712/0 (Power=1.0)
Number Cards

      Number cards are a type of asset. Ownership of number cards is
      restricted to players and contracts. Each number card has a type,
      which is a decimal digit in the range 0 to 9 inclusive. Number
      cards with the same type are fungible.
      
      The Numerator is an office, and the recordkeepor of number cards.
