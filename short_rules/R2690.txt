Rule 2690/3 (Power=1.0)
Spendies

      The Spendor is a sortitioned office.
      
      Spendies are a fungible liquid asset ownable by players and
      contracts. Spendies are tracked by the Spendor in eir weekly
      report.
      
      At the end of each month, all Spendies are destroyed. At the
      beginning of each month, every player is granted 20 Spendies.
