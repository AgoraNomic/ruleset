Rule 2699/0 (Power=1.5)
Contests

      Contestation is a secured negative boolean contract switch tracked
      by the Notary. A contract whose contestation switch is true is a
      contest.
      
      A player CAN flip a specified contract's contestation to true
      without 2 objections.
      
      A player CAN flip a specified contract's contestation to false
      with Agoran consent.
